Trail Explorer

A responsive Idaho hiking experience for discovering trails and planning future adventures.

Project Overview

Trail Explorer is a simple, outdoor-focused website that helps hikers browse Idaho trails, search for a suitable route, and keep a list of hikes they want to complete. The layout and content structure are shaped by the two wireframes provided in the project assets.

Wireframe Inspiration

Idaho Hikes Page

This page is designed as a trail discovery view with a clear heading, search tools, a filter area, and a list of trail cards.

Planned Hikes Page

This page focuses on saved or planned hikes, presenting a more compact list for users who want to revisit favorite routes.

Site Structure

Page 1: Idaho Hikes

  • Hero heading and short intro
  • Search box and filter controls
  • Trail cards with distance, difficulty, and region
  • Call-to-action to save or plan a hike

Page 2: Planned Hikes

  • List of saved or planned trails
  • Simple cards with trail name and status
  • Quick access to details or remove from the list
  • Clean layout for easy review

Interaction & Content Notes

  • Use clear badges for easy, moderate, and hard trails.
  • Show key details such as mileage, elevation gain, and location right on each trail card.
  • Keep the planned hikes page visually lighter and more list-focused than the discovery page.
  • Prioritize mobile-friendly spacing and large tap targets for filters and save buttons.
